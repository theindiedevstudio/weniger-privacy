# Weniger — Privacy Policy

  **Last updated:** March 28, 2026

  ## Overview

  Weniger ("Less, but better") is a minimal weekly planner app. Your privacy is simple: **we don't collect any data**.

  ## Data Storage

  All your data — tasks, settings, recurring templates, and preferences — is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. Nothing is sent to any server, cloud, or third party.

  ## Data We Do NOT Collect

  - No personal information
  - No email addresses or accounts
  - No analytics or tracking
  - No crash reports sent externally
  - No advertising identifiers
  - No location data
  - No contacts or phone data
  - No cookies or web tracking

  ## Notifications

  Weniger uses local notifications for:
  - Focus timer alerts (when a Pomodoro session ends)
  - Daily planning reminders (if enabled by you)

  These notifications are **scheduled locally on your device**. No data is sent to any push notification service.

  ## Third-Party Services

  Weniger does **not** use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. There are no ads.

  ## Data Export & Deletion

  - You can export all your data as a JSON file from Settings → Export all data
  - You can delete all your data from Settings → Clear all data
  - Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently

  ## Children's Privacy

  Weniger does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
